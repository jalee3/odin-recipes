Beef Pepper Lunch Recipe

Description

If you have ever been to Asia, you might have heard of a popular fast food chain called pepper lunch. That is the place that popularized this beef pepper rice. Itâ€™s very simple, just sliced beef, rice, corn, green onion, and a sweet & salty sauce to go with it served on a sizzling hot plate.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs thinly sliced ribeye
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 2 stalks green onion
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Prepare your ingredients by first soaking your frozen corn kernels in hot water for 10 seconds and thinly slicing your onion and green onion.
  2. Pre-mix the sauce.
  3. Heat up your cast-iron pan over high heat then add 1 tbsp of oil.
  4. Place sliced onions in the middle of the pan then begin to place the sliced beef along the side of the pan
  5. Add your rice on top of the onions and add the sauce on top.
  6. Add butter, corn, green onions, egg (optional), and mix everything together unitl combined.

Reference: Kwokspots Beef Pepper Rice
